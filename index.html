How this works

You're standing in the middle of a 360° room. Here's how to get around:

  1. Look around. Click and drag anywhere to turn and explore the room in every direction — things are all around you, not just in front.
  2. Start at the green marker. When you begin, look for the glowing green marker — that's your starting point. Click it to open the welcome and get going.
  3. Explore the glowing markers. Around the room you'll find gold glowing markers, each a new topic. Click any one to open it.
  4. Finish each topic. When you complete one, its marker becomes a gold checkmark ✓. Topics you haven't unlocked yet appear dimmed with a 🔒 — keep going and they'll open up.
  5. Reach the Final Message. Work through everything in the room. Once every section is complete, the Final Message unlocks — that's the finish line.

Your progress is shown in the bottom-left corner. Take your time and explore!
